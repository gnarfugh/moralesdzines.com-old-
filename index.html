About

It is what you make it.
-Atilano Klidaras

I graduated from Purdue Univeristy in 2011 and moved to Chicago. I have a passion for learning and I'm constantly pushing myself to acquire new skills. Web Development is such a fast paced industry with new technologies, frameworks, and languages coming out everyday, that it's pretty easy to fall behind.

So that's what I've been doing. I will never get better unless I make it happen. I've found that in order to grow you must continue and explore. You need to put yourself in new situations to become better. I drive myself to create new ways of thinking and develop every day. There is always room for improvement. If you do not believe that, you will start to feel too comfortable with yourself. Comfort deprives innovation. It forces you to rely on what you HAVE instead of what COULD BE. In technology, everything can and should always be better.

Including Me.
